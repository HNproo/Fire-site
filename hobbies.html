Hobbies

Chess:

I started playing chess in March of 2023 and in that time I managed to climb up to 1757 ELO on chess.com which puts me in the 1.5 percentile of players. I originally started playing chess as a competition with a friend of mine, after racing each other back and forth eventually, I managed to come out on top, that long lasting competition resulted in chess being more than just a board game for me, it became memories with a friend. The thing I love about that game is that it teaches you to take responsibilty for your own actions and what i mean by that is when you lose a match, it's no ones fault except your own, at some point you made a mistake, it might be a move you didn't see, a tactic from an opponent you missed or a piece you blundered, in one way or another it's your fault.
